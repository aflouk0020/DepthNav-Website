Research Team

The four-member team behind the DepthNav Research project

DepthNav Research is a collaborative MSc project focused on evaluating architecture-driven trade-offs in monocular depth estimation under edge deployment constraints. The team combines research, experimentation, validation, implementation, and technical reporting across the full project lifecycle.
