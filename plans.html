Research Plans & Protocol Structure

Global locks, sprint structure, and inherited experimental plans

The DepthNav Research project is organised around a master locked protocol that governs dataset control, reference baselines, reproducibility rules, naming conventions, and CSV-based evidence generation. All sprint plans inherit these global locks, while extension plans remain subordinate to the core study and do not redefine official benchmark conclusions.

Technical Appendix Position

Detailed protocol elements such as SHA256 hashes, full CSV schemas, freeze conditions, processor bypass rules, pinned revisions, and risk management logic are treated as technical appendix material. The website summarises those rules in a structured and readable form, while the full experimental protocol remains part of the project’s formal documentation and reproducibility archive.
