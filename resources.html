Reproducibility Summary

All experiments were conducted under a controlled protocol using locked datasets, frozen reference baselines, fixed naming conventions, and CSV-generated reporting outputs.

  • Master video locked by SHA256
  • Frozen 1080p reference baselines
  • CSV files as single source of truth
  • Cross-device validation across RTX, M1, and Raspberry Pi
